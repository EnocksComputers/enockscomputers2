Who We Are

Kampala's Neighbourhood Tech Shop

Enock's Computers started in 2012 when founder Enock Ssekandi opened a small repair bench in Kampala's CBD with one goal: give Ugandans access to affordable, reliable computer services without the runaround.

What started as a two-person operation fixing laptops for university students has grown into a full-service tech hub handling everything from corporate IT contracts to CCTV installations. Today, our team of certified technicians serves thousands of clients — students, entrepreneurs, NGOs, and businesses — across Kampala and beyond.

We believe technology should work for every Ugandan, not just those who can afford to fly their devices to Nairobi or pay premium import prices. That's why we keep our pricing honest, our turnaround fast, and our quality high.
